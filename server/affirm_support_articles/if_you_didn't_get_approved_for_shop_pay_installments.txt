If you didn't get approved for Shop Pay Installments

If you didn’t get approved for Shop Pay Installments, then check your email inbox for an email from Affirm. This email provides more information about your application. Keep in mind that you might be approved the next time you apply. 

If you can’t find the email, then: 
  Confirm you're checking the same email account that you use for Shop Pay
Check other email folders, such as your Spam or Junk folder
