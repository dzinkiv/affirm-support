About soft credit checks

A soft credit check is performed when you create an account with Affirm. While it may appear on your credit report, a soft credit check doesn’t impact your credit score. 

You can dispute this soft credit check with TransUnion if you believe there was fraud involved or if you’re not the owner of the account that was opened. Click here to initiate the dispute with TransUnion or contact them at 1-800-663-9980. They'll work with Affirm to investigate your dispute and you'll be notified by TransUnion when the dispute is resolved.
