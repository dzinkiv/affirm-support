View your installment balance and payment schedule

You can see your balance at affirm.ca or in the Affirm app:
  At affirm.ca:
  Sign in to your Affirm account.
Navigate to Loans.
Select the loan.
See the loan schedule and remaining balance. 
  In the Affirm app:
  Download the app for free from the App Store (iOS).
Sign in to your account.
Tap Manage at the bottom of the screen.
See the purchase balance at the top of the screen, or tap a purchase to see its remaining balance and other details.
