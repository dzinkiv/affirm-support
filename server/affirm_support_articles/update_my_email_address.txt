Update my email address

  Follow this link to update your email address.

You can also update this information by signing in to your account in the Affirm app or at affirm.ca.
  On mobile in the in the Affirm app:
Open the Affirm app and sign in to your account.
Tap the profile icon with your initials at the bottom right of the screen.
Tap the Email field showing your current email address.
Enter your updated email address.
Tap the checkmark.

On desktop at affirm.ca:
Sign in to your account at affirm.ca.
Click the profile icon with your initials at the top right of the screen.
Click Settings.
Click the Email Address field showing your current email address.
Enter your new email address.
Click Save.
