Where is my refund?

To see the status of your refund:
  Sign in to your Affirm account.
Navigate to Loans.
Select the loan.
Your refund will show up as an adjustment on the loan schedule.
  Don’t see it? Your refund will show up on your loan balance within 3–5 business days.
  Still waiting? We can check on that refund for you. Just fill out the form below.
